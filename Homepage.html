History of the club

The club was officially founded on 29th November 1899. It grew from strength to strength, winning titles such as the Macaya Cup in 1902, the Pyrenees Cup in 1910 - 1912. Barcelona FC built their first stadium in 1909 with a capacity of 6,000 people. During the 1920's the team's fame and success grew.
